About


After working for two years as a web developer in the health tech field, I am looking for something more creative. My goal is to be a part of a great, collaborative team where we can build innovative projects together. I can tell you that I am always ready to put in some elbow grease to expand my knowledge on tools of the trade.

Currently, I am enrolled at Sheridan College for a post-graduate certificate in Interactive Media exploring interaction and UX design. I also have a B.Sc. degree in Cognitive Science and Psychology from the University of Toronto and completed the full stack web development course at Bitmaker Labs. I'm available for new opportunities in April 2018. If you would like to know more, let's get in touch!
